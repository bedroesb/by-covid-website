The BeYond-COVID project aims to make COVID-19 data accessible to scientists in laboratories but also to anyone who can use it, such as medical staff in hospitals or government officials.

Pursuing to go beyond SARS-CoV-2 data, the project will serve as the groundwork to make data from other infectious diseases open and accessible to everyone.
</div>


## The challenge

The world has generated vast amounts of data in response to the COVID-19 pandemic, and still is generating more. However, identifying, connecting and integrating these for effective analysis remains a challenge.

<h2 class="mt-10">The outcomes</h2>

The project will make data about SARS-CoV-2 and other viruses easier to access, aggregate and analyse. This will enable scientists to respond faster to new strains of SARS-CoV-2 or to new viruses. It will also help policy-makers assess the impact of the disease and take the most appropraites measures to protect people against new viral diseases.
